Welcome

You can expect to find a friendly, come-as-you-are welcome from folks at First Christian Barbourville. You also will find a mix of people who are seeking to grow in their relationship with Jesus Christ; to worship in ways that blend the past with the present; to share God’s love through service to neighbors in our community; and, to find ways to bear witness to God’s grace in their lives. You might say we are an intergenerational fellowship on a journey together to live into what St. Augustine said about those who want to know more about God’s grace, "You have made us for yourself, O Lord, and our heart is restless until it rests in you."

Our Confession

As members of the Christian Church, we confess that Jesus is the Christ, the Son of the living God, and proclaim him Lord and Savior of the world

Our Mission

To go to all peoples and to make them Jesus’ disciples, to baptize them in the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, and to teach them to obey everything Christ commanded (Matthew 28:19-20)

Our Vision

  • GATHER for worship inspired by Scripture and the Holy Spirit
  • GROW in discipleship to Jesus Christ
  • SERVE our neighbors in response to God’s love
